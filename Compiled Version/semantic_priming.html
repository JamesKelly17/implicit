Brand Association Study

Today you will be playing a matching game.

During the game, brand logos will appear in the middle of your screen.


When the logo matches the top left brand, press the E key as fast as you can. When it matches the top right brand, press the I key as fast as you can.

When the logo matches the top left brand, press the Left button as fast as you can. When it matches the top right brand, press the Right button as fast as you can.

If you make an error, a red X will appear. Correct errors by hitting the other key/button.

Please try to go as fast as you can while making as few errors as possible. If you are going too slow, a "Too Slow" message will appear.

Game Structure

There will be 3 rounds to the game.

  • Round 1: will be a training round to practice
  • Round 2: will be the official start of the game. Your scores will count in this round!
  • Round 3: will be the same as before. But this time, random words will briefly appear before the real words. Remember, your task will stay the same. Keep matching the logos like you did before!

Good luck! Remember to go as quickly as you can.
